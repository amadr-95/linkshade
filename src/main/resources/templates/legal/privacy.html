Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Linkshade (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to LinkShade using Google OAuth, we collect the following information necessary to verify your identity and provide our service:

  • Email Address: Used to identify your account and for communication.
  • Profile Information: We access your public profile name and avatar to personalize your experience within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following specific purposes:

  • To authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In.
  • To provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of the App.
  • To prevent fraud and ensure the security of our users.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may only disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Google User Data Compliance

Our App's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
